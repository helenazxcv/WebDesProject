About

The Author and Blog




Jana Pauline is a 22-year old college student. She grew up in the Middle East, a place steeped in history, beautiful culture, and fine cuisine. Jana developed her passion for food and cooking at a young age being exposed with Middle Eastern cuisine. She started her vegan journey at the age of 19. She also wears cruelty-free products, from clothing to cosmetics and personal care. She is currently living in the Philippines to pursue her tertiary studies. Jana has three pets: a pomeranian, a siamese cat, and a persian cat. Her family still lives in Dubai, UAE and visits them often during breaks. Aside from clean eating, she also has an active lifestyle as she goes to yoga and pilates classes.

The blog's theme is about animal cruelty in all forms. The author will be writing blog entries to expose the truth about animal cruelty and how abhorrent it is.

The blog will also feature vegan recipes from the author to share her knowledge and inspiration for food.

The author is also down for fashion. Through sustainable materials, fashion has become even better. The blog will also feature fashion lookbooks and makeup tutorials using cruelty-free cosmetics.
