Love Animals? then Quit Fashion!

Animals are being brutally killed all in the name of fashion.

Many animals have died for the clothing industry. Their skins are used to manufacture leather, wool, and fur clothing. Animals are not ours to wear!

Cruelty in the Name of Beauty

Animals are forced to undergo lab procedures that causes them pain, suffering, and distress.

Animals like mice, rats, rabbits, dogs, cats, and primates are being locked in cages in laboratories all over the world. They suffer in extreme pain, loneliness, stress, frustration, and even death. Animals are not ours to experiment on!

Do Not Eat Your Friends

Veganism is about compassion, not restriction.

The meat industry's widespread animal abuse causes 56 billion farm animals to die every year. Slaughterhouses perform abusive methods as animals are shackled upside down, paralyzed, drowned, then killed. Not only do animals die, but the environment also suffers a devastation. Animals are not ours to eat!

Humiliation is Not Entertainment

Animals are not actors or circus clowns for spectacle shows.

For the sake of entertainment, animals are forced to perform silly and confusing tricks under the threat of being whipped and punished. These animals are carted across different countries and are placed in stuffy boxcars or trailers. They are chained and caged in filthy enclosures. Animals are not ours to use for entertainment!

Hunting is not a Sport

Animals are not trophies!

Animals, especially endangered and exotic ones, are being hunted, killed, and treated as trophies. Most victims include lions, tigers, giraffes, elephants, bears, rhinoceroses, and deers. Animals are not objects! It may be a sport to you, but it's called life to them.
